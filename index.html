What do they entail?

Web development

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites or web applications that are accessed through the internet or an intranet.It is divided into:

  1. Frontend development

    2. Focuses on the visual part of a website — what users see and interact with. Technologies used are HTML (structure), CSS (styling), JavaScript (interactivity), Frontend frameworks like React, Vue, or Angular

  2. Backend development

    3. Deals with the server, databases, and application logic — the behind-the-scenes part that powers the frontend.

Data Science

Data science is the field of study that combines statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to extract meaningful insights from data.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, programs, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage. It's all about keeping digital systems secure — from personal devices to large corporate or government systems.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is the branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines or systems that can simulate human intelligence — like learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.
